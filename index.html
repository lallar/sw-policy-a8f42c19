SuperWino Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

SuperWino is a music player for iPhone and iPad that connects to user-provided Navidrome/Subsonic-compatible music servers.

Information We Collect

SuperWino does not collect, sell, or share personal information with the developer.

The app does not include advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Information Stored on Your Device

SuperWino may store app settings, server connection details, cached library metadata, artwork, lyrics, playback state, favorites, ratings, downloads, and other music-library data on your device to provide the app's features.

This information is used locally by the app.

User-Provided Music Servers

When you connect SuperWino to a Navidrome/Subsonic-compatible server, the app communicates directly with the server you provide. Your server address, login credentials, library metadata, and playback requests are handled between your device and your chosen server.

SuperWino does not operate or control those servers.

Third-Party Services

Some optional app features may contact third-party services to retrieve music-related information, such as lyrics, artwork, animated artwork, or related-track data. These requests may include music metadata such as artist name, album name, or track title.

These services are used only to provide the requested app features.

Downloads and Offline Playback

If you download music for offline playback, those files and related metadata are stored locally on your device by the app.

Children's Privacy

SuperWino is not intended to collect personal information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL.
